WINDOW / CREATIVE_PROCESS.LOG

Creative Process

We start with strange ideas, test them quickly, and keep the parts that feel surprising. Our process is messy, playful, and focused on discovering moments that players will remember.

01

Prototype the weird idea

Start from the mechanic that sounds too strange to pitch normally.

02

Break the system

Push the prototype until the funny accident appears.

03

Find the fun

Keep the strange part only when it becomes satisfying to play.

04

Build the world

Turn the mechanic into characters, places, rules, and jokes.

05

Polish what matters

Make the experience readable without sanding away the personality.

Behind the Scenes

Devlog 04

We accidentally made the slime too powerful.

The prototype started as a small movement test. Then the slime learned to launch itself across traffic, climb buildings, and emotionally damage street lamps.

Concept Note

The city should feel annoyed, not evil.

The world reacts like a tired office worker, not a villain.

Prototype Test

Players kept throwing trash at the moon.

So we made it a feature. The moon now has opinions.
